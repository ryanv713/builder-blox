How the Marketplace Works

This section ties into your assignment: inventory, orders, and customer accounts.

Inventory Management

When a customer places an order, the marketplace reduces the stock level for each Builder Blox set. The business can restock, add new products, or remove items that are no longer available.

Order Processing

Orders move from the shopping cart into the order list. The business can view pending orders, mark them as shipped, and keep basic order history for future reference.

Customer Accounts

Customers can create accounts, log in, and see their previous orders. Password resets and simple profile updates would be handled in this part of the system.
